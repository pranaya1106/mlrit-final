Cover Story

621 offers, ₹51 LPA highest — inside the season that broke records.

The numbers are loud — but the stories behind them, of late-night DSA grinds, mock interviews with alumni and a midnight pep-talk that turned the season, are louder.

By MLRIT Chronicles • Apr 12, 2026 • 8 min read

FILE PHOTO · Graduates at the 21st Annual Day, where Sri Bala Prasad Peddigari spoke on academics-meets-industry.

MLR Institute of Technology closed its placement season with 621 confirmed offers across engineering, sciences and management — the highest tally in its twenty-year history, and a ₹51 LPA package as the season's headline number.

Behind the announcement is a routine that began long before the recruiters arrived. Students kept a four-week sprint log of DSA problems shared on a Slack channel; alumni in Bangalore ran weekly mock interviews on Sunday mornings; the placement cell built a public dashboard that anyone could open to see who was on the floor that day.

"We stopped treating placement like a season and started treating it like a habit," said one fourth-year coordinator. "The number is just the receipt."
