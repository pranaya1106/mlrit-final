Attainment Methodology
How CO & PO Attainment is Computed

CO attainment is calculated at the end of each semester using a weighted combination of direct and indirect assessment scores. PO attainment is derived from CO attainment using the CO–PO mapping matrix.

  • CO Attainment = (80% × Direct Score) + (20% × Indirect Score)
  • PO Attainment = Σ (CO Attainment × CO–PO Mapping Strength) / Σ Mapping Strengths
  • Target: At least 60% of students must achieve ≥60% marks in each CO
  • Mapping Scale: 1 = Low correlation, 2 = Medium correlation, 3 = High correlation
