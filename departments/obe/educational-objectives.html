Outcome Based Education
Educational Objectives & Outcomes
Computer Science & Engineering — MLRIT, Dundigal
Programme Educational Objectives
PEOs — B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PEO 1
Graduates of the program will have a globally competent professional career in the software industry.
PEO 2
Graduates of the program will pursue higher education and research.
PEO 3
Graduates of the program will have entrepreneur skills to solve societal problems.
Programme Outcomes
POs — B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PO 1
Engineering Knowledge: Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems.
PO 2
Problem Analysis: Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences.
PO 3
Design/Development: Design solutions for complex engineering problems and system components considering public health, safety, cultural, societal, and environmental factors appropriately.
PO 4
Conduct Investigations: Apply research-based methodologies including experimental design, data analysis, and information synthesis to reach valid conclusions.
PO 5
Modern Tool Usage: Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of their limitations.
PO 6
The Engineer and Society: Apply contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal, and cultural issues relevant to professional engineering practice and responsibilities.
PO 7
Environment and Sustainability: Comprehend professional engineering solutions' societal and environmental impacts and recognize the necessity of sustainable development.
PO 8
Ethics: Apply ethical principles and maintain professional ethics, responsibilities, and norms of engineering practice.
PO 9
Individual and Team Work: Demonstrate individual competence and function effectively as a member and leader in diverse, multidisciplinary teams.
PO 10
Communication: Communicate complex engineering activities effectively — through reports, design documentation, and presentations — with diverse audiences.
PO 11
Project Management: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of engineering and management principles and apply them to manage projects in multidisciplinary environments.
PO 12
Life-long Learning: Recognize the need for and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.
Programme Specific Outcomes
PSOs — B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PSO 1
Master software system structures and development methodologies to tackle contemporary computing challenges.
PSO 2
Develop intelligent and autonomous systems to cater societal needs in health care, e-commerce, banking, agriculture, and cyber security.
Programme Educational Objectives
PEOs — M.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PEO 1
Graduates will excel in their professional careers or higher education by acquiring in-depth knowledge and research skills in advanced computing technologies.
PEO 2
Graduates will continuously enhance their knowledge and research capabilities to match developments in the IT industry and pursue innovative or entrepreneurial initiatives.
PEO 3
Graduates will exhibit professionalism, cultural awareness, teamwork, ethics, and effective communication in solving social and environmental problems using advanced computer technology.
Programme Outcomes
POs — M.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PO 1
Scholarship of Knowledge: Independently acquire, assimilate, and apply knowledge at the forefront of the discipline for research and development.
PO 2
Critical Thinking: Apply analytical thinking, evaluation, and synthesis to solve complex technical problems from multiple perspectives.
PO 3
Problem Solving: Think innovatively, laterally, and originally in complex and applied situations and identify problems and possible solutions.
PO 4
Research Skill: Plan and execute original research, interpret results, and draw well-reasoned conclusions using scientific methodology.
PO 5
Usage of Modern Tools: Create, select, apply, and adapt appropriate techniques, modern tools, and technologies to conduct research and development of complex systems.
PO 6
Collaborative and Multidisciplinary Work: Work effectively in teams within a multi-disciplinary setting and apply their skills and knowledge to the work of the team.
PO 7
Project Management and Finance: Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of financial, project management, and business engineering principles.
PO 8
Communication: Communicate complex technical and research matters effectively to diverse audiences — technical and non-technical.
PO 9
Life-long Learning: Engage in continuous learning and keep up-to-date with evolving technologies and research trends.
PO 10
Ethical Practices and Social Responsibility: Apply ethical norms to professional activities and demonstrate awareness of societal responsibilities as an engineer.
Programme Specific Outcomes
PSOs — M.Tech Computer Science & Engineering
PSO 1
Apply advanced research methodologies and computational techniques to solve real-world problems in areas like AI, Machine Learning, Data Science, and cybersecurity.
PSO 2
Design and develop scalable, secure software systems and publish research findings in reputed national or international journals and conferences.
